About TaskFlow 💡

A lightweight, privacy-first task manager that lives entirely in your browser.

🎯

What is TaskFlow?

TaskFlow is a clean, modern to-do application designed to help you stay organized without the friction. No accounts, no servers, no clutter — just you and your tasks.

All your data lives in your browser's localStorage, which means it's always private, always fast, and works completely offline.

⚙️

Technologies Used

Built with the fundamentals — no frameworks, no dependencies.

🌐 HTML5 🎨 CSS3 ⚡ JavaScript (ES6+) 💾 localStorage API 🔡 Google Fonts
✨

Key Features

  • Add tasks with optional date & time reminders
  • Mark tasks complete with a single click
  • Separate views for active, completed & reminder tasks
  • Overdue task highlighting
  • Filter tasks by type
  • Persistent storage — survives page refresh
  • Responsive — works on mobile & desktop
  • No login, no server, 100% private
📐

Design Philosophy

TaskFlow follows a dark productivity aesthetic — deep charcoal tones with warm amber accents for focus without eye strain.

Typography uses Syne for bold display headings and DM Sans for clear, readable body text.

UI patterns include CSS Grid/Flexbox layouts, smooth micro-animations, and card-based organization for visual clarity.

🔒

Privacy & Data

TaskFlow stores all your task data locally in your browser using the localStorage API under the key taskflow_tasks. Your data never leaves your device. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. Simply open the browser, and your tasks are right where you left them.

⚠️ Note: Clearing your browser's localStorage or site data will permanently delete your tasks. Consider exporting important data before clearing browser storage.
